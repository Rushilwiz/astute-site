Value Added Services

Astute offers a comprehensive portfolio of services tailored for every stage of a power system life cycle, whether it is design, construction or operation and maintenance. Our services integrate and optimize the elements of a power system for deriving maximum benefits. We help our clients in keeping power systems safe, efficient, reliable and up-to-date. We diagnose problems, identify ways to improve performance, transform concepts into practical solutions which leads to better productivity and higher capital utilization. We offer several value added services like:

Energy Audits

We have the expertise to help you successfully integrate green measures into your power system. We perform energy audits, analyze quality of input power, identify external & internal sources of impurity and provide optimal corrective solutions. We identify wasteful patterns in total consumption of electrical and other forms of energy and present recommendations that provide cost-effective solutions to shrink energy usage.

Forensic Investigations

We have extensive experience in performing failure analysis for systems in industrial, commercial, institutional and infrastructure projects. We form our opinions based upon the facts discovered through detailed, systematic investigations and draw meaningful results/conclusions using our thorough knowledge and experience. We then recommend robust improvements to minimize/prevent recurrence of similar failures.

Value Engineering

Our Value Engineering services help in discovering inefficiencies in your project plan and developing economical solutions.

We develop low cost alternative solutions while maintaining quality, reliability and functionality of the system. We think outside the box to achieve the most cost effective solution that suits your budget and meets with your needs.

Peer Review

We provide a third eye in reviewing your project drawings and specifications to verify that all information is properly indicated, documents are coordinated amongst all disciplines, information is clear & concise and meets highest quality standards. It is statistically proven that savings derived from such reviews more than pay for the fees incurred to conduct them.

Factory Witness Testing

We accompany/represent our customers during factory testing to ensure that the system matches the design and that the manufacturer gets opportunity to rectify errors in fabrication, component selection, assembly and configuration prior to shipment. We identify and correct manufacturing errors at the factory, which is more effective, efficient and economical than doing it in the field.
