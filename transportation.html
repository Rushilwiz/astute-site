Transportation

Astute offers services for a variety of projects involving complex electrical engineering in the transportation segment. Our engineering services range from baseline or feasibility studies to fully developed engineering reports & comprehensive, bid-ready design documents. We also provide construction period services, quality assurance/quality control (QA/QC) services, witness factory/field testing and support services for construction engineering & inspection (CEI) contracts for transportation industry.
