Government

Astute provides engineering services for various initiatives and projects undertaken by federal government agencies such as the General Services Agency (GSA), courts and Judiciary Service buildings, government office buildings, central power plants housing steam & chilled water systems, Army, Air Force & Naval establishments, etc. We work with state agencies like the Department of General Services, city & county government offices, and safety & support services like Police Stations, Fire Stations, and other administrative offices.

Our broad range of services include system studies, condition assessment, design of upgrades, modifications & new projects and oversight of installations, inspections and testing & commissioning. We provide robust systems ensuring availability of reliable power to the facilities.
