Utilities & Renewable Energy

Astute provides electrical engineering services for diverse range of market segments like Electric Utilities, Transmission & Distribution Companies, Municipalities, Gas, Water & Wastewater Utilities and Renewable Energy (PV Solar) plants for public & private sector undertakings. Our experienced engineers are highly qualified and equipped to handle projects of all sizes and complexities from the initial concept to its successful completion. We ensure that each of your project specific needs are satisfied.

Water/Wastewater Treatment Plants & Pumping Stations

Our designs ensure that continuous, safe and reliable electrical power is available to these vital plants so that you can rest assured that you get uninterrupted supply of treated potable water and that your wastewater is reliably dispensed.

Substations & Distribution Stations:

We design safe, sleek & efficient systems for handling, transmission & distribution of power at high, medium & low voltages with optimal levels of redundancy to ensure reliability and continuity.

Renewable Energy Plants

Through our designs of PV Solar system installations, we make our contribution towards reducing carbon footprint for a sustainable society. We focus on efficiency and strive to optimize the utilization of the system.
