Services

Astute's team of electrical engineers with over 50 years of experience is capable of handling all of your electrical engineering needs. Astute undertakes assignments for studies & analyses, engineering & designs, with drawings and specifications.

Astute has the knowledge, skills, tools & systems and most importantly, has practices in place to perform the required tasks. It is our resolve to ensure thorough compliance with all applicable codes and standards such as the National Electrical Code (NEC), National Fire Protection Association Code (NFPA), International Electrical Testing Association Standard (NETA), National Electrical Manufacturing Association (NEMA) standards, International Building Code (IBC), International Energy Conservation Code (IECC), ASHRAE, IESNA Lighting etc., in everything we do. All our electrical designs are performed with the core principle of meeting and exceeding the operational, maintenance, energy usage and budget goals of our clients.

Our goal is to design safe, reliable and sustainable electrical systems with flexibility for growth and changes throughout the useful life of the system.
