Power Distribution Systems

Our highly experienced electrical engineers have designed numerous complex power distribution systems including high, medium and low voltage systems for customers in various industry segments. Our designs address all power system requirements ensuring safety, reliability and flexibility. We professionally assess the condition of existing power system, diligently analyze and develop efficient designs that optimize capital investment & operating expenses. Whether you are looking for basic design consultation, feasibility studies or a complete turnkey solution, Astute will work with you on your endeavors and take it to fruition.

Brief listing of our engineering expertise:

  • Industrial Power Distribution Systems including Substations
  • Building Power Distribution
  • Switchgear/Substation Modernization, Automation and/or upgrades
  • Emergency Standby Generator Systems, Paralleling Switchgear & Controls
  • Uninterruptible Power Systems
  • Underground Distribution Systems
  • Power Monitoring and Metering
  • Power Conditioning, Sag Ride Through (SRT) Systems
  • Lightning, Grounding and Surge Protection Systems
  • Lighting System and Controls
  • PV Solar Systems
