Field Services

Astute offers a comprehensive portfolio of field services tailored for every segment of a power system. Our services integrate and optimize the elements of a power system to make sure it is aligned with design criteria and your business goals. Our strong team of power system experts help make your power system safe, efficient, reliable, and up-to-date. We help our clients diagnose problems, identify ways to improve performance, or transform concepts into flexible, practical solutions that can lead to better efficiencies and capital utilization.

Our power system assessment services are aimed at reducing your facility's risk from Reliability issues, Process disruptions, Code violations, Outdated equipment, and enhance workplace safety.

Power Quality Analysis

We perform power monitoring and measurements to detect symptoms of problems and deficiencies, analyze data and provide recommendations to improve power quality & system performance.

Power System Grounding Evaluation

Lack of proper grounding often results in failures or accidents, data & equipment losses, process anomalies, plant shutdowns, and workplace safety hazards. We perform studies and recommend mitigating measures to improve grounding efficiency, effectiveness and overall system safety & reliability.

Equipment Condition Evaluation, Risk Assessment and Contingency Planning

Electrical distribution equipment is subject to degradation due to age, environmental stresses, and wear & tear. We perform a thorough equipment condition evaluation by visual inspections & testing and provide recommendations for refurbishment, repair, retrofitting or replacement, as appropriate for your business.

Inspections

We perform equipment and system inspections to verify/confirm congruence with design documents and compliance with codes, standards and document & report discrepancies/deficiencies noticed.

Field Testing Oversight

We have discovered several issues during field testing oversight, which significantly benefited owners and contractors. We develop testing plans, assist testing company during complex testing and analyze test results. Our hands-on equipment handling experience puts us heads & shoulders above our competition. We witness factory and field testing of equipment to ensure your precious assets are thoroughly tested and examined at all stages. Our diligent inspection has prevented major setbacks & problems for our clients.
